# Pearson correlation

**Pearson** measures **linear** comovement: do two series go up and down together after you account for scale?

## Limits

It does **not** bend time. In our pipeline it complements [[DTW]] — often **after** warping or on aligned segments — so it captures “moving together” once shapes are comparable.
