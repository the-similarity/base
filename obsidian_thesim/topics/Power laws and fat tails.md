# Power laws and fat tails

**Fat tails:** extreme moves happen **more often** than a bell curve predicts. **Power laws** describe tails where the chance of huge events decays slowly.

## Why it matters

Risk, stress testing, and **self-similar** modeling all hinge on tail behavior. Our [[Bempedelis power-law match|Bempedelis]] lens explicitly looks for coherent scaling structure.
