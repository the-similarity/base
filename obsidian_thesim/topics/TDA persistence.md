# TDA persistence

**Topological Data Analysis** asks: what **connected pieces**, **loops**, and **holes** persist when you **thicken** a point cloud? **Persistence diagrams** summarize births/deaths of those features.

For time series, we usually build a cloud via **[[Takens embedding]]**.

## Why we use it

“Shape of the shape” — invariant to many nuisances. Literature links persistence summaries to **regime stress** and **crash-risk signals** (treat as research, not prophecy).
