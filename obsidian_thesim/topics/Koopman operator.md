# Koopman operator

**Idea in one breath:** turn **nonlinear** motion into **linear** motion **in a smarter coordinate system** (observables). Then **eigenvalues** tell you **growth/decay** and **oscillation speeds** — a “fingerprint” of the **engine** behind the data.

**DMD / EDMD** are practical ways to estimate that operator from samples.

## Why we use it

Two price windows might **look** different yet be driven by similar **dynamics**. Spectrum comparison senses that. Our [[Forecast cone in plain English|forecast cone]] can also blend in a Koopman-style forward view.
