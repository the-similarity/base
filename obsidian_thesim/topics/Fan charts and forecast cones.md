# Fan charts and forecast cones

**Fan charts** nest **percentile bands** so you see **central tendency** and **uncertainty** together — popular in central banking (e.g. Bank of England style).

Our **[[Forecast cone in plain English|forecast cone]]** is the same *idea*: show **many plausible futures** from analog outcomes, not one line.
