# Calibration and coverage

If we advertise a **90% band**, we want reality to land inside **~90%** of the time **in honest tests**. If not, we’re **overconfident**.

**Coverage** = how often truth falls in the promised interval. **Calibration** = whether **probabilities mean what they say** on repeat.
