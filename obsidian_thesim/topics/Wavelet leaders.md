# Wavelet leaders

**Wavelets** zoom in and out on **time vs frequency**. **Wavelet leaders** summarize how **roughness / singularity** behaves across scales — a **multifractal fingerprint**.

## Why we use it

Two periods can look similar to the eye yet differ in **how volatility clusters across horizons**. Leader-based spectra catch that.
