# Transfer entropy

**Question:** does knowing **past A** reduce uncertainty about **future B**, beyond knowing **past B alone**?

It is **directional** (A → B) and picks up **nonlinear** influence better than classical Granger in general.

## Why we use it

Other methods say “**similar**.” Transfer entropy adds: “**informative for what’s next?**” — a **quality filter** on matches.
