# Matrix Profile

The **matrix profile** records, for every subsequence, the distance to its **nearest neighbor** elsewhere in the series. **STUMPY** is the standard fast, exact implementation family.

## Why we use it

**Parameter-light**, exact nearest-neighbor structure for subsequences — great for **motifs** (repeating patterns) and **discords** (weird segments). Often used in **Tier 1** to surface strong candidates.
