# EMD and IMFs

**EMD** peels a signal into **IMFs** (Intrinsic Mode Functions): roughly, **simple oscillations** from fast to slow, plus a **trend**.

## Why we use it

Two windows can match on **slow trend** but not **fast ripples** (or the reverse). Matching **layer by layer** catches that.

**CEEMDAN** reduces **mode mixing** — a common EMD headache.
