# Bempedelis power-law match

**Plain-language idea:** two windows might *look* related only on the surface. **Bempedelis-style tests** ask whether both obey a convincing **power-law scaling** relationship — a stricter notion of **structural** self-similarity than “similar wiggles.”

Good fits get credit; **noisy, overfitted** scaling does not.
