# Backtesting and walk-forward

**Backtest:** pretend you’re at some past date, run the pipeline **only on data before that moment**, predict ahead, **compare** to what really happened.

**Walk-forward:** slide that window **forward** many times — avoids **one lucky split**.

## Why it matters

Without it, confidence is **storytelling**. With it, you learn if bands are [[Calibration and coverage|calibrated]] and if edges survive **new** periods.
