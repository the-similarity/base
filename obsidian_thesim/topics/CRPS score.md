# CRPS score

**CRPS** scores **whole predictive distributions**, not just hits/misses. Lower is better. It rewards **sharp** forecasts **and** **calibration** in one framework.

Gold standard for comparing **probabilistic** forecasts — we cite it in our [[Engine map|backtesting metrics]] stack.
