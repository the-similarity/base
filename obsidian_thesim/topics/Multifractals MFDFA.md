# Multifractals MFDFA

Monofractals use **one** scaling exponent. **Multifractals** admit **different** scaling for **small vs large** fluctuations — closer to real markets.

**MFDFA** generalizes DFA to a **family** of moments \(q\), yielding **\(h(q)\)**, **\(\tau(q)\)**, and a **singularity spectrum** \(f(\alpha)\).
