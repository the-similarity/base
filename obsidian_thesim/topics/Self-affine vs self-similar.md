# Self-affine vs self-similar

**Self-similar:** stretch **time and value** by the **same** factor.

**Self-affine:** needs **different** scaling for **time** vs **price** — the usual situation in finance.

Tools like **DFA / MFDFA** are tuned for **self-affine** behavior; naive fractal shortcuts can mislead.
