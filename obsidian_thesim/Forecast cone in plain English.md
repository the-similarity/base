# Forecast cone in plain English

After we find **similar historical windows**, we look at **what happened right after** each match. Those “next chapters” are combined into **bands of possibility**:

- A **middle path** (often the 50th percentile — “typical” outcome among analogs)
- **Lower and upper envelopes** (e.g. 10th and 90th percentiles) showing **spread** — how wide outcomes were

Visually this looks like a **fan** or **cone** widening into the future.

## Why bands matter

A single line forecast pretends certainty. Real data rarely behaves that way. **Bands communicate uncertainty** honestly — if they are [[Calibration and coverage|calibrated]].
