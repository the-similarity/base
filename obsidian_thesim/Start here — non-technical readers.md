# Start here — non-technical readers

This vault explains **The Similarity**: software that searches history for patterns similar to today’s market window and shows **what happened next**, with **uncertainty bands** (a “cone” of possibilities).

## How to use this with an LLM

Give the model access to this Obsidian folder (or paste note exports). Then ask questions in normal language, for example:

- “What is [[DTW]] and why do we use it?”
- “Explain [[Koopman operator]] like I’m new to math.”
- “What is [[Walk-forward validation]] and why does it matter for trusting forecasts?”
- “How is this different from TradingView?” → [[Product story and competition]]

The notes are **linked**: follow `[[double bracket]]` links in Obsidian, or ask the LLM to “follow the links” between topics.
