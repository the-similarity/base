# Product story and competition

**What we build:** arbitrary **pattern search** (not only named chart shapes), **multi-lens similarity**, and **probabilistic** “what happened next” — see [[The question we answer]].

## The gap in the market (simple view)

Many tools either:

- recognize **fixed templates** (head-and-shoulders, triangles), or
- use **one** similarity metric (e.g. correlation only), or
- find neighbors but **don’t** build a full **forecast cone + evaluation story**.

We combine **several independent views** of “sameness,” then aggregate evidence into a structured outcome view. See [[Why nine lenses]].
