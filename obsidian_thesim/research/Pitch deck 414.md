## Core framing

**The Similarity** is a structural intelligence engine.

It finds when the present rhymes with the past, scores analogs structurally instead of visually, and projects what tends to happen next with honest uncertainty.

## Pitch spine

1. Problem
   - current forecasting tools are shallow, black-box, or narrow
2. Solution
   - structural matching + probabilistic projection
3. Wedge
   - finance first
4. Moat
   - engine depth + calibration + platform reuse
5. Expansion
   - [[Synthetic Data]]
   - 3D latent exploration
   - world-event prediction
   - natural language -> time series

## One-line differentiator

**Not just shape matching. Structural matching.**

## Key slide messages

- The engine is the moat.
- Finance is the proving ground.
- The primitive is bigger than finance.
- Honest uncertainty matters more than fake certainty.
- Platform breadth comes from one reusable core: [[Self-similarity]].
