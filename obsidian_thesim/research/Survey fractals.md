## Friendly summary

Markets **rhyme across zoom levels** and throw **bigger shocks** than bell curves predict. Fractal / **multifractal** tools measure **how** that scaling behaves.

## What we extracted

- **Power-law tails:** extreme moves matter; “Gaussian comfort” is false for returns.
- **Hurst / memory:** trending vs mean-reverting **flavor** (careful — estimators differ).
- **DFA & MFDFA:** robust scaling exponents; **multifractal spectra** for richer reality.
- **FMH:** stability as **multi-horizon** participation story.
- **Self-affine:** realistic finance is **not** uniform zoom in time vs price.
