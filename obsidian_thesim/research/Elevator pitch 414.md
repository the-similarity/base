# Elevator pitch 414

## 15-second version

"We’re building The Similarity, an engine that finds when the present rhymes with the past, scores those analogs structurally instead of visually, and projects what tends to happen next with honest uncertainty."

## 30-second version

"The Similarity is a structural intelligence engine for time series. Instead of relying on generic black-box forecasts or shallow chart analogs, we match patterns across multiple mathematical lenses, then build calibrated forecast cones from what actually happened next. We start in finance, but the same primitive expands into synthetic data, world-event prediction, and natural-language-to-time-series."

## 60-second version

"The Similarity is based on a simple thesis: self-similarity is the primitive. We built an engine that searches history for structurally similar patterns, not just visually similar ones, using a multi-method pipeline across dynamics, topology, spectra, and self-similarity. From those analogs, we generate forecast cones with explicit uncertainty instead of pretending the future is a point estimate. Finance is our first wedge because it gives us dense data, fast feedback, and customers who pay for edge. But the deeper opportunity is much bigger than finance."

## Positioning lines

- "Not just shape matching. Structural matching."
- "A similarity engine for forecasting under honest uncertainty."
- "We find what rhymes across time, domain, and scale."
- "Self-similarity is the primitive. Everything else is an application layer."
