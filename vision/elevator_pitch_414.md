# The Similarity — Elevator Pitch 414

## 15-second version

"We’re building The Similarity, an engine that finds when the present rhymes with the past, scores those analogs structurally instead of visually, and projects what tends to happen next with honest uncertainty. We start in finance, where the feedback loops are fast and the willingness to pay is high, but the same primitive expands into synthetic data, world-event prediction, and natural-language-to-time-series."

## 30-second version

"The Similarity is a structural intelligence engine for time series. Instead of relying on generic black-box forecasts or shallow chart analogs, we match patterns across multiple mathematical lenses, then build calibrated forecast cones from what actually happened next. We’re starting in finance because it’s the best place to prove edge, but our long-term vision is much bigger: synthetic data, 3D latent state exploration, world-event prediction, and turning human narrative into analyzable time series."

## 60-second version

"The Similarity is based on a simple thesis: self-similarity is the primitive. We built an engine that searches history for structurally similar patterns, not just visually similar ones, using a multi-method pipeline across dynamics, topology, spectra, and self-similarity. From those analogs, we generate forecast cones with explicit uncertainty instead of pretending the future is a point estimate. Finance is our first market because it gives us dense data, fast feedback, and customers who pay for edge. But the bigger opportunity is that the same engine can power synthetic training data, event forecasting, cross-domain state exploration, and narrative-to-time-series products."

## One-line positioning options

- "Not just shape matching. Structural matching."
- "A similarity engine for forecasting under honest uncertainty."
- "We find what rhymes across time, domain, and scale."
- "Self-similarity is the primitive. Everything else is an application layer."

## When talking to investors

Lead with this sentence:

"We are building a new kind of intelligence engine: one that finds when the present rhymes with the past and turns that primitive into forecasting products."

Then immediately add:

"We start in finance, but the real company is bigger than finance."

## When talking to quants or technical investors

"Think of it as an analogue-search and projection engine with a deeper structural stack than normal time-series tools. The moat is the combination of retrieval, scoring, calibration, and eventually world-model-driven projection."

## When talking to non-technical investors

"We help people answer a hard question: when has something like this happened before, and what happened next? We do it in a way that is more explainable than black-box AI and more rigorous than chart pattern folklore."
