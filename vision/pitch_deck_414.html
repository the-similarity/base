The Similarity

Self-similarity is the primitive. Everything else is an application layer.

What We Are

A structural intelligence engine that finds when the present rhymes with the past and projects what tends to happen next with honest uncertainty.

Where We Start

Finance first. Engine first. Platform later.

"We are building a new kind of intelligence engine: one that finds when the present rhymes with the past and turns that primitive into forecasting products."

The Problem

Too Shallow

Most tools rely on simple indicators, visual analogs, or fragile heuristics.

Too Black-Box

Generic models are hard to interpret and often poorly calibrated under regime change.

Too Narrow

Most systems solve one surface problem instead of building a reusable primitive.

The gap is not "more forecasts." The gap is structurally grounded forecasting with explainability and usable uncertainty.

The Solution

Not just shape matching. Structural matching.

  • Search history for analogous subsequences
  • Score them across multiple mathematical lenses
  • Project forward using what actually happened next
  • Express outcomes as calibrated ranges, not fake certainty

DTW asks if two patterns look alike. Our engine asks if they are driven by the same deeper structure.
